No Bake Keto Cheesecake

This is the ultimate keto no-bake cheesecake! It is creamy, rich, and decadent. This dessert is one of my favorites so I hope you enjoy!

INGREDIENTS

For the crust

  • 2 cups Almond Flour
  • 1 tablespoon Cinnamon
  • 1 stick of melted butter
  • Sugar substitute to taste.

For the filling

  • 1 pint heavy cream
  • 3 8oz packages of cream cheese softened
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Sugar substitute to taste.

For the topping

  • Mashed blackberries
  • 1/4 cup of Water
  • Sugar substitute

INSTRUCTIONS

For the crust

  1. In a pan brown the almond flower on medium heat.
  2. Once the almond flour is browned place in a bowl
  3. Add melted butter and cinnamon to the almond flour
  4. Stir the ingredients together.
  5. Poor into the pie pan and even it out in pan.
  6. Place in refrigerator to cool

For the Filling

  1. In a mixing bowl whip the heavy cream until it holds peak. Add sugar substitute to taste.
  2. In another bowl add lemon juice to cream cheese,and mix together. Add sugar substitute to taste.
  3. Fold the whipped heavy cream into the cream cheese.
  4. Add the filling to the pie pan on top of the cooled crust.

For the Topping

  1. Add water, sweetener, and mashed blackberries to a pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Simmer until liquid reduces and has thickened.
  3. Let cool and add to the top of filling.
  4. Refrigerate for 2 hours or more and enjoy!

Notes

For best results I have found that Truvia Stevia Leaf works best for sugar substitute.
