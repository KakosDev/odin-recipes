Perfect Scrambled Eggs

The perfect scrambled eggs recipe that will make you feel like a kid again.

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat skillet on high heat.
  2. Add desired amount of butter to skillet.
  3. Mix the 6 eggs in a bowl or measuring cup.
  4. Add desired amount of salt to mixed eggs.
  5. Ensure the butter has browned before pouring eggs into skillet.
  6. Cook eggs in skillet until they are done and enjoy!
IMPORTANT NOTES
  • Make sure the eggs are mixed before they're poured into skillet. If they're mixed in skillet they wont have the same texture.
  • Also ensure the butter is browned for the flavor, and the pan needs to be very hot before cooking the eggs. If they are slowly cooked it will also change the texture.
