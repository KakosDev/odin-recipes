Broiled Cinnamon Toast

Easy to make warm, delicious, and crispy cinnamon toast. It will melt in your mouth.

INGREDIENTS

  • White bread
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar or sugar substitute
  • Butter

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Pre heat the oven using broil at 500 degrees or high setting.
  2. Place bread on a baking sheet.
  3. Place 5 slices of butter on bread. 1 in center and 1 in each corner.
  4. Mix sugar and cinnamon in a spice shaker
  5. Shake the cinnamon-sugar onto the bread until desired amount is achieved.
  6. Place in oven around 8 inches from top of the oven.
  7. Keep an eye on it, and remove when its toasted to your liking.
  8. Throw on a plate and Enjoy!
